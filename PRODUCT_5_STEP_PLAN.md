# BTBworkflow Product 5-Step Plan

## Step 1 — Input Standardization
- Support local dataset uploads
- Support schema-driven field mapping
- Validate required and expected fields

## Step 2 — Template Generalization
- Support multiple reusable business templates
- Allow template-specific expected fields and schema hints

## Step 3 — AI Insight Structuring
- Produce findings, risks, and recommendations
- Keep AI outputs grounded in prepared data

## Step 4 — Delivery Layer
- Provide chart configs
- Provide image output
- Provide HTML export

## Step 5 — Product Workspace
- Organize datasets and templates as reusable assets
- Control workflow execution from the dashboard
- Keep the repository clean for reuse and publication
